About Me

I am a highly motivated professional specializing in **digital accessibility** and inclusive design, holding a Bachelor of Management Studies degree. My primary focus is rigorously applying **WCAG 2.2 principles** (Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust) to ensure seamless and equitable digital experiences for all users. I possess practical expertise in utilizing **Semantic HTML, ARIA, and CSS** to fix barriers and enhance usability for assistive technologies. Furthermore, I am proficient in manual accessibility testing using screen readers like **NVDA** (for web) and **TalkBack** (for mobile apps), ensuring every user, regardless of ability, can fully navigate and understand digital content.
